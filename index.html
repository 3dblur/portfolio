About

Hi, I'm Hasan Syed a product designer based out of India, i love designing experiences, and creating innovative products, i have been designing since 2020, for creative reasons at first that quickly turned into designing for purpose

I love designing for web3, being in the industry as a user first, led to a lot of challenges i new firsthand that had to be overcome owing to the lack of intuitiveness in tech.

In highly technical products jargon doesn't need dumbing down, its communication needs to be levelling up the user and meet midway.
